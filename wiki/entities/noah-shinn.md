# Noah Shinn

**Noah Shinn** is a researcher at Northeastern University. He is the first author of [[reflexion]], a framework for verbal reinforcement learning in LLM agents.

## Key Contributions

- **[[reflexion]]** (2023) — introduced verbal self-reflection as a lightweight alternative to gradient-based RL for LLM agents. Achieved 91% pass@1 on HumanEval (surpassing GPT-4's 80%) and near-perfect 130/134 on ALFWorld.
- **LeetcodeHardGym** — a code-generation RL benchmark of 40 challenging Leetcode problems in 19 programming languages, introduced alongside Reflexion.
