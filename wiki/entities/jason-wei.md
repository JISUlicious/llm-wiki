# Jason Wei

**Jason Wei** is a researcher at Google Research (Brain Team). He is the first author of [[chain-of-thought-prompting]], one of the most influential papers on eliciting reasoning in LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- **[[chain-of-thought-prompting]]** (2022, NeurIPS 2022) — showed that including step-by-step reasoning in few-shot exemplars unlocks multi-step reasoning in large language models. Demonstrated this is an emergent ability of model scale (~100B+ parameters). Achieved SOTA on GSM8K and multiple reasoning benchmarks.
- **Emergent Abilities of Large Language Models** (Wei et al., 2022b) — work on characterizing abilities that appear only at scale, referenced throughout the CoT paper as framing for why CoT only works with large models.
