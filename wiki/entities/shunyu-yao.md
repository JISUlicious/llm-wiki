# Shunyu Yao

**Shunyu Yao** is a researcher at Princeton University's Department of Computer Science, working with Karthik Narasimhan. He is the first author of [[react]], one of the foundational papers on combining reasoning and acting in LLMs.

## Key Contributions

- **[[react]]** (2022, ICLR 2023) — introduced the paradigm of interleaving reasoning traces and actions in LLM prompting. Demonstrated that reasoning + acting synergistically outperforms either in isolation across QA, fact verification, text games, and web navigation.
- **[[reflexion]]** (2023, co-author with [[noah-shinn]]) — verbal reinforcement learning framework that extends ReAct with self-reflection and episodic memory. Achieved 91% pass@1 on HumanEval and 97% on ALFWorld.
- **WebShop** (Yao et al., 2022) — an online shopping benchmark environment with 1.18M real-world products, used to evaluate interactive decision-making agents.
